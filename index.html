Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your bánh mì different?

We focus on authentic Northern Vietnamese flavours, which are lighter, more balanced, and less sweet. Everything is built around fresh ingredients and bread baked daily at 5am!

Is your bread really baked fresh every day?

Yes! Our bread is baked fresh every morning at 5am. That’s what gives our bánh mì its crispiness on the outside and the fluffiness on the inside.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer vegetarian options like tofu bánh mì. Just let us know your preference when ordering.

Can I customise my order?

Of course. You can adjust things like chilli, coriander, or sauces to suit your taste. Just tell us when you order.

Is your bánh mì greasy and heavy?

We focus on fresh ingredients and balanced flavours. Our bánh mì is designed to feel light and satisfying, not overly heavy or greasy.
